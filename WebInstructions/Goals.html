Add New Event:

Click Add New Event Result: When dialog window opens choose from the Stroke and Distance Drop down menus.

Enter Time as Minute:Second:100ths of Second: EX 01:12:67

Choose Date from calendar select dialog.

To create, after information is filled in, click the SUBMIT button.

Add Personal Goal:

Click Current goal link or Add under Goal Time. When dialog window opens choose from the Stroke and Distance Drop down menus.

Enter Time as Minute:Second:100ths of Second: EX 01:12:67

To create or update after information is filled in, click the SUBMIT button.
